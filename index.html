Our Story

A Sanctuary of
Self-Care & Style

Founded in 2018, Lumière Salon was born from a passion for transformative beauty experiences. We believe that luxury isn't just about the services we offer—it's about how you feel from the moment you walk through our doors.

Our team of internationally trained stylists and estheticians combine technical expertise with artistic vision to create looks that enhance your natural beauty. Using only premium, sustainable products, we ensure that every treatment nourishes both you and the environment.
